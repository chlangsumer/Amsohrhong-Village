In this is Website you can find some informations related to my village.My Village was located near the border of Bangladesh which was just few killometres to reach Bangladesh.It is about 120 kms far from Shillong and about 60km from Jowai.

The village is named after one big river "Amsohrhong" which lies in the north position of it."Am" means water and "Sohrhong" means a fruit that present around of the village.


In our village mostly people spent their times as farmers and some as daily labourer. They used to grow crops like betelnut, betel-leaf and broomstick which are the main parts of their jobs here.Growing fruits also is the part of their living. Fruits like lemon, jackfruit, litchi, mango, pineapple, and so on are present here.

Fishing is one of their hobbies which they used to spent after their works. They used to go for fishing once in a week.


Literacy rate in this viilage is very low.It has only one school under SSA scheme.
